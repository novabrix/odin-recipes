Chicken Alfredo

A quick and easy chicken alfredo dinner with broccoli, zucchini, and red bell pepper. The sauce is enriched by cream cheese.

Total estimated cook time: 40 minutes

Simple, yet effective!

Ingredients needed:

  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Cream cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Spices
  • Boneless Chicken
  • Fresh Broccoli
  • Zucchini and Bell Pepper

      • Steps required:

      1. Boil the pasta al dente
      2. Melt cheese and butter, and add Xtra Ingredients to the pot
      3. Cook 3 minutes over medium heat, let simmer for 5 minutes
      4. Serve over fettucine
