Homemade Lasagna

This home-cooked dish was made with tomato sauce from scratch and a delicious cheesy filling. With one bite, you'll have to stop yourself from not making more!!

Total estimated cook time: 3 hours

But worth every bite!

Ingredients needed:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar and Spice
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

      • Steps required:

      1. Cook the meat
      2. Cook the noodles
      3. Make the cheese layer
      4. Assemble the lasagna
      5. Bake the lasagna
